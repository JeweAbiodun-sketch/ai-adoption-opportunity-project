**Key insight for Cleo:** The gap between "using AI" (89%) and "fully implemented" (33%) is the opportunity. Most mid-market retailers are experimenting but not yet capturing value. The window to gain competitive advantage is open — but closing fast.

## 3. Mid-Market Retail Specific Signals

- Average retail margin 2–5% — operational inefficiency is material at this scale
- Retailers with 500–5,000 SKUs are the sweet spot for AI demand forecasting viability
- 42% of retail AI projects fail due to data quality — fragmented POS, CRM and inventory systems are the #1 risk
- Affordable SaaS AI vendors now serve mid-market at £500–£5,000/month (Inventory Planner, Relex Lite)
- Mid-market retailers lack data volume of large chains but have more implementation flexibility

## 4. Hype Signals to Watch

| Claim | Hype Level | Reality |
|---|---|---|
| "AI will replace your buying team" | High hype | AI assists buyers, rarely replaces them |
| "Plug-and-play AI inventory" | Medium hype | Data prep typically takes 3–6 months |
| "Personalisation from day one" | Medium hype | Requires 6–12 months unified customer data history |
| "AI chatbots reduce CS costs by 80%" | High hype | 30–50% realistic; 80% requires mature implementation |

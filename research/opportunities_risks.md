# Opportunities, Risks & Hype Mapping — Mid-Market Retail AI
**Project 5 · Sector: Retail · Company Size: Mid-Market (51–250 employees)**

---

## Opportunity Map

### Opportunity 1 — AI Demand Forecasting & Inventory Optimisation ⭐ Recommended
**Evidence strength:** High — multiple published case studies at mid-market scale
- Inventory cost reduction: 10–20%
- Overstock and stockout reduction: 30–50%
- Payback period: 9–18 months
- Entry-level vendors: Inventory Planner (£99/month), Relex Lite, Blue Yonder Luminate
- **Why first:** Seasonal complexity and margin pressure make this the highest-ROI entry point. Works on existing ERP/POS data — no customer data platform required.

### Opportunity 2 — Customer Personalisation & Recommendations
**Evidence strength:** Good, conditional on data readiness
- Revenue uplift: 15–25% on targeted channels
- Repeat purchase rate improvement: 10–15%
- **Prerequisite:** Unified customer data (12+ months history)

### Opportunity 3 — Dynamic Pricing & Markdown Optimisation
**Evidence strength:** Moderate, growing
- Margin improvement: 3–8%
- Clearance sell-through improvement: 20–40%
- **Risk:** Customer perception risk if implemented poorly

### Opportunity 4 — AI Customer Service & Returns Automation
**Evidence strength:** Good
- Cost reduction: 30–50% on tier-1 queries
- Returns processing time reduction: 40–60%
- **Best for:** High-returns retailers (fashion, electronics)

---

## Risk Map

| Risk | Severity | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Poor data quality blocks implementation | High | High | Data audit before vendor selection |
| Staff resistance and workaround behaviour | High | Medium | Change management from day one |
| Legacy POS/ERP integration complexity | High | High | Pilot on one store or channel first |
| Vendor lock-in | Medium | Medium | Choose open API vendors, retain data ownership |
| Over-investment in wrong use case | High | Medium | Use diagnostic tool to identify fit first |
| Budget overrun on implementation | Medium | High | Fix budget at pilot phase; scale only on evidence |

## ROI Estimation (Mid-Range Scenario)

**Assumptions:**
- Annual revenue: £15M
- Current inventory holding cost: 25% of stock value (~£1.5M annually for a £6M stock base)
- AI achieves 15% reduction in inventory waste (conservative based on published evidence)
- Gross margin improvement from fewer markdowns: 2 percentage points

| Benefit | Annual Value |
|---|---|
| Inventory holding cost reduction (15%) | £225,000 |
| Reduced markdown losses (2% margin on £15M) | £300,000 |
| Reduced stockout lost sales (estimated 1% of revenue) | £150,000 |
| **Total estimated annual benefit** | **£675,000** |
| Annual cost (mid-range) | £21,600 |
| **Net annual benefit** | **£653,400** |
| **ROI** | **~30:1** |

*These are estimates based on published retail AI benchmarks. Actual results depend on data quality, implementation quality, and market conditions. Cleo should validate with a 3-month pilot before committing.*

# Datasets — Download Instructions

## Dataset 1: Retail Sales Dataset (Primary)
**URL:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mohammadtalib786/retail-sales-dataset
**Filename to save as:** `retail_sales_dataset.csv`
**Columns:** Transaction ID, Date, Customer ID, Gender, Age, Product Category, Quantity, Price per Unit, Total Amount

**Download steps:**
1. Go to the Kaggle URL above
2. Click "Download" (requires free Kaggle account)
3. Save the CSV to `data/raw/retail_sales_dataset.csv`

## Why These Two Datasets?

**Retail Sales Dataset:**
- Demonstrates the type of transactional data that feeds AI demand forecasting
- Contains seasonal patterns, category distribution, and customer demographics
- Used in Tableau: Sheet 2 "Your Sales Data: Why AI Would Help"

**AI Tool Adoption Dataset:**
- Shows AI adoption rates across industries including retail
- Used in Tableau: Sheet 3 "Hype vs Evidence" — retail position on adoption curve
- Helps Cleo benchmark her business against sector averages
